Privacy Policy

At Lead Needle, we take your privacy seriously. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect your name, phone number, service address, and service preferences when you request a quote or schedule an appointment through one of our client businesses.

2. How Information Is Used

We use this information solely to connect you with the local service business you've requested to hear from. Our automated system (AI assistant) may send follow-up messages to help you schedule and confirm your appointment.

3. Opt-In & Consent

By submitting your information via a form, ad, or direct conversation, you are consenting to receive SMS messages related to your request. You can opt out at any time by replying STOP.

4. Sharing of Information

We only share your information with the service provider you requested. We never sell or distribute your data to third parties.

All the above categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties.

5. Data Protection

We follow industry best practices to keep your information secure and prevent unauthorized access.
